Privacy Policy

Aegis Boost · Effective date: May 24, 2026

Overview

Aegis Boost is an Android utility app designed to help users view device health, storage, memory, battery, app activity, and optimization information.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may access, how it is used, and what choices you have.

Information We Access

Aegis Boost may access device-level information needed to provide app features, including:

  • Battery level, charging status, temperature, and health information.
  • Storage usage and available storage space.
  • Memory/RAM usage and availability.
  • Installed app information and app permission indicators.
  • Basic device diagnostics used to display health and optimization insights.

This information is used to show device status, scan results, optimization summaries, notifications, and activity history inside the app.

Local Storage

Aegis Boost stores some app preferences locally on your device, such as onboarding completion, notification settings, theme settings, and recent activity events.

This local data stays on your device and is used only to make the app function correctly.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, Aegis Boost may send local device health reminders, battery alerts, optimization reminders, and daily status summaries.

You can disable notification categories from the app settings or from Android system settings.

Advertising

Aegis Boost may show ads using AppLovin MAX mediation and Google AdMob. These advertising partners may collect or receive certain information from your device, such as advertising identifiers, device information, app interaction data, and approximate usage signals, in order to provide, measure, and improve ads.

Third-party advertising partners process data according to their own privacy policies:

  • AppLovin Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Data Disclosure Information

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, address, phone number, or email address unless you contact us directly.
  • We do not read your personal files, photos, messages, or private content.
  • We do not claim to detect viruses, malware, hacking, or threats beyond the app’s stated utility features.
  • We do not sell personal information directly to third parties.

App Permissions

Aegis Boost may request permissions required for app functionality, notifications, advertising, network access, and device information features. Permissions are used only for the purpose described by the related app feature.

Data Sharing

We do not manually share your personal information with third parties. However, third-party SDKs used for advertising, analytics, or platform functionality may process data as described in their own policies.

Children’s Privacy

Aegis Boost is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

  • You can disable notifications in Android settings or inside the app.
  • You can reset or limit your Android advertising ID in your device settings.
  • You can uninstall the app at any time to remove locally stored app data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
